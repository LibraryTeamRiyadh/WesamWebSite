About FAJER AMALY

Fajer Amaly, founded in 2020 and headquartered in Riyadh Fajer Amaly is a Saudi Systems Integrator & IT Solutions/Services company. specializes in IT Infrastructure, data center; Physical & Cyber Security, Storage, Systems, low current, audio video and Networking. Our Team deploys these solutions in Traditional IT Enterprises, Private, Public & Hybrid Clouds and IoT & Edge Environments. Fajer Amaly provides expert support for all of the solutions we offer including design, implementation and managed services. Fajer Amaly serves customers in the Private & Public sectors and has become a trusted partner to increase the effectiveness of how IT positively impacts business.
